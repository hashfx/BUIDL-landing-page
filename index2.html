Why BUIDL Crew?

The BUIDL Crew is a dedicated space for developers that offers a wide range of opportunities for developers to learn, grow, and build.

  • Special invite-only access to developer meetups & events
  • Exciting monthly contests with equally exciting prizes
  • Early access to all the latest Lumos Labs Product updates
  • Special Incentives from the Lumos Metaverse for contributions
  • Premium job offers from our partners
  • Mentorship, guidance, and support from top industry experts

How to join Lumos Metaverse BUIDL Crew

  1. Apply for the Lumos Metaverse whitelist and mint your Lumos Metaverse NFT (ignore if already done)
  2. Join the Lumos Labs Discord Server
  3. Go to the #buidl-crew channel and click on the "Join BUIDL Crew" button
  4. Complete the BUIDL Crew application form
  5. Await to be reviewed by the BUIDL Crew team
  6. If you are accepted, you will be granted access to the BUIDL Crew community and all of the benefits that it offers
